Yin Minn Pa Pa

Specially Appointed Associate Professor
Yokohama National University

I study cyberattacks to understand what is happening, who is behind them, and what we can do to stop them. I observe real-world attacks through honeypots and internet-scale monitoring, and investigate cybercriminal activity across the web, blockchain, social media, and hidden communities on the dark web. I connect these different sources of evidence, using AI and large language models where appropriate, to produce actionable threat intelligence. I then explore how this intelligence can be used in practice, from notifying potential victims and reporting abuse to relevant platforms and service providers, to measuring whether these actions make a real difference.
